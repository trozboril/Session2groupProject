We are tapt.

A social media web application which brings together owners of bars serving craft beers, right to the people who love to drink them. Tapt provides for an owner of an establishment the power to change their inventory and let the beer drinkers know what is currently on tap.

The general user of Tapt is allowed to rank beers they have come across on their travels, rank the establishment itself, and tag beers they are interest in and be notified when the beer is back on tap. Users can also find resources on Tapt to educate them about all of the terrific beer types that exist in the world.

Did we also mention that you can create a customized pub crawl for you and your friends to share with the world? Yeah, we did it. You're welcome.
